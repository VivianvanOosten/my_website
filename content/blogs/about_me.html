I am Vivian van Oosten, an enthusiastic data scientist interested in learning more about machine learning applications.

Academics

I majored in Economics and Mathematics for my undergraduate, with a focus on statistics and data science. My favorite course was called Data Analytics for Liberal Arts and Sciences. This was taught by alumni from the programme, who had a passion for data science. The course was a great introduction to the field and sparked my interest to study data science further. Following that, I have taken courses in Machine Learning and Applied Data Science.

Work experience

To experience what working with data is like in a company, I interned at Stater, a mortgage services firm in the Netherlands. I was part of their Data Lab that experiments with data and tries to automate processes and provide insights to the business. I learned the importance of legibility, version control and documentation. As part of the internship, I have applied best practices in terms of code organisation to various applications (Flash and R-Shiny).
